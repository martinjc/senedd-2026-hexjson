Senedd 2026 Hexmaps

This page shows two hexmaps of the Senedd 2026 constituencies, the first where each constituency is represented by a single hexagon, and the second where each constituency is represented by six hexagons (one for each seat in the constituency). Click on a constituency to see the name of the constituency. Boundaries between constituencies are also represented in the data. Hexjson data files are available below and in the repository
